PrismPeak Server Ranks

Beginner

As a Beginner, you start your journey in the PrismPeak server. Access to basic commands and areas to help you get started.

Explorer

Explorers have proven their skills and gained access to advanced commands and new areas to explore. They also earn more rewards.

Adventurer

Adventurers are seasoned players who have access to exclusive content, special events, and enhanced abilities.

Elite

Elites are top-tier players with the highest privileges, including powerful commands, VIP zones, and unique perks.

Moderator

Moderators help maintain order on the server. They have the ability to manage players, enforce rules, and assist in server events.

Admin

Admins oversee all server activities, manage the community, and ensure the server runs smoothly. They have full access to all commands and settings.
